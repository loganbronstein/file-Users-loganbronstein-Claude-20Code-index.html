## 1. THE CORE REFERRAL STRUCTURE

### Recommended Structure: $50 Cash + Friend Discount

**Referrer gets:** $50 cash (Venmo, Zelle, or check) for every referred client whose items sell.

**Referred friend gets:** 5% off the commission rate on their first Sale Advisor job.

**When rewards trigger:** When the referred client's first item sells and the client gets paid. Not on booking. Not on the consultation. On the actual sale.

**Why this is the move:**
- Cash is universally motivating. Nobody turns down $50.
- The friend gets a tangible discount, lowering the barrier to try.
- Tying reward to a completed sale means you only pay out when you've made money.
- Set a minimum job value of $200 in total sale value to keep the math clean.

**Keep them engaged during the wait:** Text the referrer when the friend books: "Your referral just booked — you're one step closer to your $50."

## 2. REFERRAL TIERS / GAMIFICATION

### Tier Names

**Tier 1: The Connector** — 1-2 successful referrals
**Tier 2: The Hustler** — 3-5 successful referrals
**Tier 3: The Dealmaker** — 6-10 successful referrals
**Tier 4: The Advisor** — 11+ successful referrals

### What Each Tier Unlocks

**The Connector (1-2 referrals):**
- $50 cash per successful referral
- Personal thank-you text from Logan
- Early access to Sale Advisor updates and new features

**The Hustler (3-5 referrals):**
- $50 cash per referral PLUS one-time $25 bonus when they hit this tier
- Their own commission rate drops 2% permanently on all future jobs
- Shoutout on Sale Advisor social media (with permission)
- Priority scheduling — their consultations get booked first

**The Dealmaker (6-10 referrals):**
- $75 cash per successful referral (raised from $50)
- Commission rate drops another 2% (4% total improvement)
- Free "mini cleanout" — up to 5 items with waived percentage
- Custom referral link (once app is built)
- Invited to quarterly "Dealmaker Dinner" — small gathering at a Chicago restaurant

**The Advisor (11+ referrals):**
- $100 cash per successful referral
- Commission rate drops another 2% (6% total)
- "Sale Advisor Ambassador" status — first dibs on partnership/employment opportunities
- Physical gift — high-quality branded item (jacket, weekender bag)
- Direct line to Logan
- Name on Sale Advisor website as a founding Chicago Ambassador

### How to Make People Want to Climb

**Visibility:** "You just hit Hustler status. 3 referrals and counting — you're in the top 10% of Sale Advisor clients in Chicago."

**Exclusivity:** The Dealmaker Dinner — 8-12 people max. Networking, social currency, something to work toward.

**Progress updates:** Monthly text: "You're 2 referrals away from Hustler status. Know anyone cleaning out their place this spring?"

## 4. VIRAL LOOP MECHANICS

### The Loop

1. Client books consultation → 2. Sale Advisor sells items → 3. Client gets paid → 4. Client is thrilled, Sale Advisor asks for referral → 5. Client refers 1-3 friends → 6. Friends book consultations → 7. Repeat

**Target viral coefficient:** 0.5+ at launch, 0.8+ by month 3. Above 1.0 = exponential growth.

### Push the Coefficient Higher

- **Make the payout shareable.** Send a graphic: "I just made $[amount] selling stuff I didn't even want anymore" with Sale Advisor branding. Screenshot-and-post worthy.
- **Make the consultation jaw-dropping.** "That lamp is worth $120, that dresser is $400, those sneakers are $200" — that moment is viral content. Ask permission to film it.
- **Follow up at the right intervals.** Ask at payout, again at 2 weeks, again at the start of next season.

### The "Before and After" Content Strategy

**The concept:** Show the transformation. Before: cluttered garage. After: clean space AND a payout screenshot.

**Execution:**
1. At every consultation, photograph the items (with permission) = "before"
2. After everything sells, photograph the clean space + payout total = "after"
3. Create post: "Sarah had 14 items she hadn't touched in 2 years. We sold all of them in 9 days. She made $1,847. Book your free consultation: [link]."

**Where to post:** Sale Advisor social, Chicago Facebook Groups, Nextdoor (geo-targeted to client's neighborhood), client's own social (with permission)

**Video version:** Walk through house with client, point at items, say "this is going to sell, this is going to sell, this one might surprise you" — cut to payout screen. This format destroys on TikTok and Reels.

### The Neighborhood Effect

When you complete a job in a neighborhood, it becomes a "hot zone":

1. **Ask the client:** "Do any of your neighbors have stuff to sell? Happy to leave a few referral cards."
2. **Post on Nextdoor:** "Just wrapped up a job in [neighborhood]. If anyone nearby has stuff to sell, we're already in the area — happy to swing by for a free consultation this week."
3. **Yard sign (with permission):** "Sale Advisor — We Sold It. [Phone Number]" — like realtor "Sold" signs.
4. **Second client on the block:** Tell both: "You're our second client on this block — [neighborhood] is keeping us busy!" Creates momentum.
5. **Hit 3+ clients in a neighborhood:** Post: "We've helped 3 families in [neighborhood] sell over $X,000 this month. Want in?"

### Leveraging Nextdoor & Facebook Groups

**Nextdoor:**
- Post in specific neighborhoods after completing a job there (mention the neighborhood name — algorithm favors hyper-local)
- Respond to every "recommendations for..." post even tangentially related
- Encourage clients to post their experience on Nextdoor (neighbor posts convert at dramatically higher rates)

**Facebook Groups:**
- Lead with value or a story, never a pitch
- Different content for different groups (buy/sell gets listing-style, neighborhood gets story-style, parenting gets "we helped a family declutter kids' stuff" style)

### Day-to-Day Process
- New lead comes in → always ask "How did you hear about us?"
- If referred → log in tracker immediately
- When booking → confirm: "Were you referred? Your friend gets $50 and you get 5% off."
- When item sells for referred client → check tracker, mark sale, send referrer reward within 24 hours

### What to Automate Later (When App/CRM Is Built)
- Unique referral links/codes per client
- Automatic tier progression notifications
- Automatic reward payouts (or flagging)
- Client-facing referral dashboard
- Automated follow-up texts at key intervals

### KPIs to Track

**Weekly:**
1. Referral conversion rate (referral → booking): Target 40%+
2. Viral coefficient: Target 0.5+ at launch, 0.8+ by month 3
3. Cost per acquisition via referral: Should be 30%+ lower than paid ads
4. Participation rate (% of clients who refer): Target 20% at launch, 35% by month 3
5. Average referred client job value

**Monthly:**
6. Time from referral to booking: Target under 14 days
7. Reward payout speed: Target within 24 hours
8. Tier distribution
9. Top referrer concentration (broad base vs. power referrers)
10. Social media mentions (track manually — screenshot every mention)

### When It's Working vs. Needs Adjustment

**Working:**
- 15-20%+ of clients refer someone within 30 days
- Referred clients booking at 35%+ rate
- Referral CPA meaningfully lower than paid CPA
- Organic social mentions without prompting
- Multiple clients reaching Hustler tier in first 2 months
- Neighborhood clusters forming

**Needs adjustment:**
- <10% of clients refer after 30 days → incentive not compelling enough or not asking at right moment
- Referrals coming but not converting → friend incentive too weak or follow-up too slow
- Only 1-2 people making all referrals → program not accessible enough
- People referring but not claiming rewards → process too confusing

**Levers to pull:** Increase cash reward, increase friend discount, simplify process, change ask timing, add limited-time bonus, personally call top 10 clients.

## 6. LAUNCH STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch Seeding (1 Week Before)

1. Identify 20 most engaged Lakeshore clients in Chicago
2. Send personal text: "I'm launching Sale Advisor and putting together a referral program for people I trust. You'd get $50 for every person you send our way. Interested?"
3. Send full details + 5 physical referral cards to anyone who responds
4. Post on personal social: "Big announcement coming next week. If you've ever said 'I should really sell that'... stay tuned."

### Phase 2: Official Launch (Day 1)

Post on all channels:
"Sale Advisor is officially live in Chicago. We come to your house, sell your stuff, and get you paid. No upfront cost. To kick it off — refer a friend: they get 5% off, you get $50 cash. No limit. Book your free consultation: [link]"

Post everywhere: Sale Advisor social, personal accounts, 10+ Chicago Facebook Groups, Nextdoor, text to 50 closest contacts.

### Phase 3: First 30 Days

**Week 1 — Activate Inner Circle:**
- Complete first 5-10 jobs. Over-deliver.
- Execute referral ask after every payout
- Post 2-3 before/after stories
- Track every referral from day one

**Week 2 — Amplify with Content:**
- Create content from real completed jobs with real numbers
- Post "first week results" summary
- Ask best clients for Google reviews
- Follow up with referred leads who haven't booked

**Week 3 — Introduce Urgency:**
- Limited-time bonus: "Double rewards this week — $100 instead of $50"
- Post the leaderboard: "Top referrer has sent 4 people. Can anyone beat that?"
- Target neighborhoods where you've already completed jobs

**Week 4 — Systematize and Assess:**
- Review all KPIs
- Thank top 3 referrers personally (handwritten note + $25 gift card)
- Identify top-performing neighborhoods, double down
- Decide if incentive structure needs tweaking
- Plan first Dealmaker Dinner for month 2

### Creating Urgency

**Founding Referrer Status:** Anyone who refers in first 60 days = "Founding Referrer" with grandfathered benefits as Sale Advisor grows.

**Monthly Contests:** Top referrer each month wins a prize ($250 cash, weekend getaway, etc.). Leaderboard updated weekly.

**Seasonal Pushes:**
- Spring: "Spring Cleaning Referral Bonus — double rewards all month"
- Back to school: "Moving? Downsizing? Refer them."
- Holidays: "Upgrading furniture for the holidays? Sell the old stuff first."
- New Year: "New year, clean house. Refer a friend and start the year $50 richer."

**Scarcity:** "We're booking 2 weeks out — but referred clients get priority scheduling. Now's the time to send friends our way."
