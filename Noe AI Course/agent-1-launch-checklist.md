- [ ] Register Sale Advisor as an LLC in Illinois

- [ ] Draft standard client service agreement (percentage, liability, timeline, unsold items, cancellation)
- [ ] Have agreement reviewed by a lawyer

- [ ] Build simple pricing calculator (Google Sheet) for walkthrough demos
- [ ] Document pricing clearly on website
