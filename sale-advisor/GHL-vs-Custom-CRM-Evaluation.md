# GoHighLevel vs. Sale Advisor Custom CRM — Evaluation

## Bottom Line

The custom CRM can do everything GHL does. GHL gives you pre-built sales/marketing automation. Your custom CRM handles the consignment-specific operations GHL will never support. The question is build time vs. monthly fees.

## GHL Strengths

- Operational in days, not weeks
- Built-in Twilio (SMS, calling, missed call text-back)
- Marketing automation out of the box (drip campaigns, follow-ups, review requests)
- Point-and-click — no code sessions needed for sales/marketing features
- A2P 10DLC verification handled faster

## Custom CRM Strengths

- No monthly fees (~$97–$497/mo saved vs. GHL)
- Full control — no platform limitations or workarounds
- Handles everything unique to Sale Advisor (cataloging, listings, deliveries, payouts)
- Grows directly into the Purchase Advisor marketplace
- One system to manage instead of two
- Every GHL feature can be built via Claude Code sessions

---

## Decision Framework

**Go GHL-only if:** You just need a sales/marketing CRM and don't have a unique operational workflow.

**Go custom-only if:** You want one system, zero monthly fees, and are willing to invest Claude Code sessions to build out the sales/marketing features (SMS, booking, automations).

**Go hybrid if:** You want GHL for lead capture and communication NOW, while your custom CRM handles operations — but accept the cost and complexity of managing two platforms.

---

## Recommendation

**Custom CRM, all-in.** The features GHL has that you don't yet (SMS, booking, follow-up automations) are buildable in a handful of sessions. You avoid $1,200–$6,000/yr in fees, keep one system, and build toward Purchase Advisor. GHL solves short-term convenience but creates long-term platform dependency for a business model it wasn't designed for.

---

## Pre-Launch Build Priority (if going custom)

1. Twilio SMS integration (two-way messaging from business number)
2. Automated follow-up sequences (lead nurturing)
3. Booking/calendar for walkthrough scheduling
4. Review request automation (post-delivery)
5. Missed call text-back

All buildable. All keep you on one platform.
